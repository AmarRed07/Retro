Retro

Purpose

There is something about the way websites were designed in the 90's that appeal to me. So, I decided to make a Retro About me website, while giving homage to classic HTML/CSS design; using technologies before our beloved React.js and Angular were born. Steve Wozniak, Bill Gates, Elon Musk, Larry Page, Sergey Brin - all of them started somewhere. Yes, even these giants had to learn HTML/CSS at some point before revolutionizing the programming world. So, kick your feet up and lets go back in time.
